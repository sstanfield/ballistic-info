Shooting angle should be entered on location to obtain the most accurate firing solution. To access this screen, use the Navigation menu.

Angle can be toggled between uphill (positive angle value) or downhill (negative angle value) by selecting the Uphill or Downhill icon. Sliders control the angle value; the top slider adjusts by units of one and bottom slider by units of ten.
